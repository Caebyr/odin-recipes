Pepperoni Pizza

Description

Pepperoni is a variety of spicy salami made from cured pork and beef seasoned with paprika and chili peppers. Prior to cooking, pepperoni is characteristically soft, slightly smoky, and bright red. Sliced pepperoni is one of the most popular pizza toppings in American pizzerias.

Ingredients

  • 1 ball of pizza dough
  • 120ml of tomato passata
  • 80g mozzarella, diced
  • 10 slices of pepperoni
  • 6 slices of chorizo
  • A pinch of dry chilli flakes
  • Flour for dusting
  • Semolina for dusting
  • A dash of olive oil for brushing

Steps

  1. Start by preparing your pizza dough
  2. Take one dough ball and place onto a hard surface that's been dusted with flour and semolina. Push down on the dough with your hands and stretch out into an even round flat shape
  3. Place your pizza base onto a pizza baking tray (10-12 inches).
  4. Spread the tomato passata over the base and top with the mozzarella cheese.
  5. Evenly place the slices of pepperoni and chorizo across the base.
  6. Place pizza into the oven at 220C for 8-12 minutes, until the crust is crisp & golden.
  7. Sprinkle over dry chilli flakes evenly.Sprinkle over dry chilli flakes evenly.
  8. Brush the crust with olive oil and enjoy!
